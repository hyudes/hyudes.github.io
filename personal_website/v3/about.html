A bit about the website - and me

My love of ice cream runs DEEP. I was born into a family that savored ice cream for breakfast on waffles or after dinner for dessert. In highschool when I was searching for an extra income, it is no suprise that I ended up working at a homemade ice cream store in Boston called White Mountain Creamery. Here, I went from newbie to connoisseur. My favorite flavor from there is called Cookie Monster, which is a combination of all of their cookie flavors. Needless to say, I have a thing for the mix-ins.
